# Merchant Dialogue
# A merchant who sells items and potions

ROOT:merchant_greeting

NODE:merchant_greeting
MSG:Welcome to my shop! I have the finest wares in the land. What can I get for you?
CHOICE:Show me your weapons. | target:merchant_weapons
CHOICE:Show me your potions. | target:merchant_potions
CHOICE:I'm just browsing. | target:merchant_browse
CHOICE:Goodbye. | target:merchant_goodbye

NODE:merchant_weapons
MSG:I have a fine Steel Sword for 50 gold, and a legendary Dragon Blade for 200 gold!
CHOICE:Buy Steel Sword (50 gold) | target:buy_steel_sword | condition:gold>=50
CHOICE:Buy Dragon Blade (200 gold) | target:buy_dragon_blade | condition:gold>=200
CHOICE:Show me something else. | target:merchant_greeting

NODE:buy_steel_sword
MSG:Excellent choice! This blade has served many warriors well.
CHOICE:Thanks! | target:merchant_greeting | gold:-50 | item:Steel Sword:WEAPON:10

NODE:buy_dragon_blade
MSG:Ah, a warrior of taste! This blade was forged in dragon fire itself!
CHOICE:Thanks! | target:merchant_greeting | gold:-200 | item:Dragon Blade:WEAPON:50 | xp:20

NODE:merchant_potions
MSG:I have Health Potions for 15 gold and Mana Potions for 20 gold.
CHOICE:Buy Health Potion (15 gold) | target:buy_health_potion | condition:gold>=15
CHOICE:Buy Mana Potion (20 gold) | target:buy_mana_potion | condition:gold>=20
CHOICE:Show me something else. | target:merchant_greeting

NODE:buy_health_potion
MSG:Here you go! This will restore 50 health.
CHOICE:Thanks! | target:merchant_greeting | gold:-15 | item:Health Potion:POTION:50

NODE:buy_mana_potion
MSG:Here you go! This will restore 30 mana.
CHOICE:Thanks! | target:merchant_greeting | gold:-20 | item:Mana Potion:POTION:30

NODE:merchant_browse
MSG:Take your time! Let me know if you see anything you like.
CHOICE:Actually, show me your items. | target:merchant_greeting

NODE:merchant_goodbye
MSG:Come back anytime, friend! Safe travels!
